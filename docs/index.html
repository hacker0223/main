⛰️ Summit
Stock research and chart practice for real people.

Summit is a stock research and education app for casual investors. It brings together live quotes, fundamentals, technicals, news, and SEC filings, plus hands-on tools for learning to read the market — without ever telling you what to buy or sell.

What's inside

  • Live market data — quotes, fundamentals, technicals (RSI, MACD, Bollinger), news, and SEC filings.
  • Chart Sandbox — practice reading candlesticks and drawing trendlines, or import a real stock's history to study.
  • Pattern Lab — see how historically similar chart shapes actually played out, with a trained model's estimate and its real backtested accuracy attached.
  • Watchlist & price alerts — kept entirely on your own device.

Built to inform, not to advise

Summit doesn't execute trades, hold funds, or make buy/sell recommendations. Every statistic comes with its real measured accuracy, and nothing in the app is financial advice — it's a tool for doing your own research more clearly.
