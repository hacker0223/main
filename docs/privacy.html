Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Summit ("we," "our," "the app") is a stock research and education app. This policy explains what data the app handles and why.

No account, no sign-up

Summit does not require you to create an account. There is no login, no email collection, and no user identity tied to your use of the app.

What's stored, and where

Your onboarding preferences (investor type, interests, risk tolerance), your watchlist, and any charts you create in Chart Sandbox are stored locally on your device only, using standard on-device storage. None of this is sent to or stored on our servers. Uninstalling the app removes it.

Market data requests

When you look up a stock, the app requests quote, fundamentals, news, and filings data from our backend, which in turn sources it from Finnhub, Yahoo Finance, and SEC EDGAR. These requests include the ticker symbol you're viewing but no personal identifiers — we don't know who's asking, only what's being asked for.

AI features (Pattern Lab)

Pattern Lab's historical pattern matching, classifier estimate, and Devil's Advocate features use Anthropic's Claude API to turn computed statistics into plain-language explanations. If you type your own thesis into Devil's Advocate, that text is sent to Anthropic's API to generate a counter-argument. It is not stored by us, and is subject to Anthropic's own privacy policy for API usage.

What we don't do

  • No advertising or ad-tracking SDKs
  • No sale of data to third parties
  • No location tracking
  • No access to contacts, camera, or microphone

Third-party data sources

Market data, news, and filings are sourced from Finnhub, Yahoo Finance, and SEC EDGAR. Each has its own terms governing the data they provide; Summit acts as a display layer over publicly available information and does not claim ownership of this data.

Children

Summit is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from them (noting again that we don't collect personal information from anyone, of any age).

Changes to this policy

If our data practices change, we'll update this page and the "Last updated" date above.
